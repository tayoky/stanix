---
title: libgfx
---
**libgfx** is the libraries of **stanix** to render graphics.  
**libgfx** include lot a of usefull functions including :
- `gfx_open_framebuffer`
- `gfx_create`
- `gfx_free`
- [`gfx_color`](gfx_color)
- `gfx_draw_pixel`
- `gfx_draw_rect`
- `gfx_clear`
- `gfx_push_buffer`
- `gfx_enable_backbuffer`
- `gfx_disable_backbuffer`
- `gfx_load_font`
- `gfx_free_font`
- `gfx_draw_char`
- `gfx_draw_string`
- `gfx_char_width`
- `gfx_char_height`
